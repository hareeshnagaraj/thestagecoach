Services
DIY Fly-By
With you, we tour your home, and offer recommendations based on your particular needs. You take notes, and do the work. We can provide hands-on assistance, if needed, for an additional fee.
Written Consultation
We provide a detailed room-by-room analysis with recommendations for the staging of your home. You can do the work, or hire us for an additional fee.
Hands-On Staging
We stage the entire house or just a room or two. Our preference is to work with your furnishings and to supplant or rent additional items only if necessary.
Real Estate Photography Assistance
We ensure a property photographs at its best. According to the National Association of Realtors, nine out of ten homebuyers begin their search online. Staging a house for a walk through and preparing it for photography are not the same. Marketing photography has become very sophisticated; with the use of fusion photography, small details -- which are sometimes overlooked -- are critical.
Everyday Living Re-fresh
Does your home need a little lift? We can help pull it together whether you've got money to spend, or not. We can give advice or take you shopping to look for the finishing touches. We'll work with one room, or the whole house.
New Home move-in
Moving into a new property can be overwhelming. We can help with furniture plans, accessory placement and paint color choice.
Downsizing move-out
We can help "downsizers" choose what furnishings and fixtures will work in a smaller home. We can also make suggestions for what to do with the items that won't be moving.
Additional Assistance
We can help in any number of ways. Give us a call!
